apama.analyticsbuilder
Event Partition_Alias

Denotes an alias device.

This is used in simulation mode and test mode to declare the real and virtual devices. In terms of checking for cycles and what model-to-model interactions are used, the realDevice member is used, but the block actually listens to events identified by the virtualDevice field.

Can be passed to BlockBase.sendsSyncOutput, BlockBase.sendsAsyncOutput or BlockBase.listensForInput to denote sending to a virtual device.
